👁️ EyeWrite — Quick Start Guide

Welcome to EyeWrite — a hands-free writing and communication tool.

Navigation:
• Hover for 1.5 s to click (Precision Mode)
• Hover for 0.7 s or single blink to click (QuickType Mode)

Controls:
• ⚡ QuickType — faster hover speed
• 🌀 Hover ON/OFF — toggle dwell clicking
• 🔊 Speak — text-to-speech output
• 💾 Save — export text (TXT, DOCX, PDF)
• 🔍 Search — open half-screen web results

Keyboard:
• Type by gaze or blink selection
• “Caps” toggles uppercase, “Shift” applies one-time uppercase

Voice Modulation:
• Adjust pitch, rate, and tone live

Exit:
• Hover away for 0.7 s or press ESC to close

EyeWrite is built for accessibility, freedom, and creative expression.
“Where the hands may rest, the eyes and voice continue to speak.”
